Recommendation

  • A (Claude coral) and B (PH orange) both reinforce the "upvote" semantic — A feels on-brand, B feels more iconic.
  • C (amber) gives a "trophy" vibe — good if ranking is the core story.
  • F (rose) is the strongest visual hit but can read as destructive since --destructive is already red.
  • D/E/G/H avoid the warm palette — cleaner separation from destructive red, but lose the "upvote" warmth.
